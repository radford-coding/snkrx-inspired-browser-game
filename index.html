a project for learning web development, inspired by a327ex's SNKRX.

rules:

Steer your snake to avoid the enemies. Defeat all waves of enemies to earn an upgraded or new unit, and progress to the next arena.

Click the settings icon to pause, change controls (wasd/arrows vs. follow mouse), mute, and adjust difficulty.

You may need to disable your ad blocker for audio.
